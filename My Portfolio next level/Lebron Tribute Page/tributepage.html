Who is Lebron James?

 
              Lebron James, also known as King James, is a professional basketball player who has played in the NBA for the 
            past 19 years, currently for the Los Angeles Lakers. Throught out his career
            he has also played for the Miami Heat and Cleveland Cavaliers.
